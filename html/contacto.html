Contact Us / Support

We're here for you every step of the way. Customer satisfaction is our #1 priority.

We try to respond to all emails within one business day from Monday to Friday. If you need to get in touch with us more urgently, you can contact us via facebook or instagram.

General Enquiries

If you have any questions or have a general queries about Bake Diary please let us know, we'd love to hear from you. You can email hello@bakediary.com.

Existing Members

If you are an existing member of Bake Diary and have a support query, please email support@bakediary.com and include your email address that you used when signing up as a reference.
